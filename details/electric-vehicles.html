Key Insights

Exponential Growth

EV market share has grown 7x since 2015, with acceleration in recent years as battery technology improves and costs decrease.

Regional Leaders

Norway leads with 80%+ EV share, while China represents the largest absolute market with millions of EVs sold annually.

Future Outlook

Projections suggest EVs could reach 15-20% global market share by 2030 as infrastructure expands and more affordable models launch.
