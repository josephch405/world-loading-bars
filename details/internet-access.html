Key Insights

Rapid Expansion

Internet access has more than quadrupled since 2005, driven by mobile technology and falling costs of smartphones and data plans.

Digital Divide

While developed nations exceed 90% connectivity, many rural and low-income areas still lack reliable access, representing a key challenge for global equity.

Future Goals

UN targets universal internet access by 2030. Satellite internet and 5G networks promise to bridge remaining gaps in remote regions.
