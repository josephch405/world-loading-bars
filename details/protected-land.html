Key Insights

Biodiversity Hotspots

Protected areas safeguard critical habitats for 80% of terrestrial species. Amazon, Congo Basin, and Southeast Asian forests represent priority conservation zones with high biodiversity value.

The 30x30 Goal

The Kunming-Montreal Global Biodiversity Framework (2022) aims to protect 30% of land and sea by 2030. This requires nearly doubling current protected area coverage in 6 years.

Indigenous Leadership

Indigenous peoples manage or have tenure rights over 40% of protected areas. These territories show better conservation outcomes and higher biodiversity levels than government-only managed areas.

Quality Matters

Not all protected areas are equally effective. "Paper parks" exist on maps but lack enforcement. Effective protection requires funding, management, community engagement, and connectivity between reserves.
