Key Insights

Early Stage Technology

L4 autonomous vehicles (fully self-driving in defined areas) are still in early commercial deployment. While millions of autonomous miles have been driven, they represent a tiny fraction of global travel.

Leading Companies

Waymo (20M+ miles), Baidu's Apollo Go, Cruise, and others are operating robotaxi services in select cities. Most deployments are geofenced to specific urban areas.

Future Growth

Projections vary widely, but some analysts predict autonomous miles could reach 1-5% of global travel by 2030-2035 as technology matures and regulatory frameworks develop.

Geographic Concentration

Most L4 autonomous miles are concentrated in a few cities: San Francisco, Phoenix, Beijing, Shenzhen, and Wuhan, with expansion ongoing.
