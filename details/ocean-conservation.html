Key Insights

The 30x30 Ocean Goal

The Kunming-Montreal Framework targets 30% of oceans protected by 2030. Current 8.2% coverage means tripling protected areas in 6 years—requiring 29.4 million km² of new marine protection.

High Seas Breakthrough

The 2023 High Seas Treaty enables protection of international waters (64% of oceans). Previously, only national waters could be protected, limiting conservation of migratory species and deep-sea ecosystems.

Large MPAs Dominate

Massive Marine Protected Areas like Papahānaumokuākea (1.5M km²), Ross Sea (1.5M km²), and UK's Blue Belt program account for much of protected ocean. Size matters for pelagic species and ecosystem resilience.

Effectiveness Varies

Only 2.6% of oceans have full or high protection (no-take zones). Many MPAs allow fishing and extraction. Effective protection requires enforcement, monitoring, and community engagement—especially challenging in remote areas.
